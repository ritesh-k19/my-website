What is Battery Swapping?


Battery swapping replaces your empty EV battery with a full one in minutes at swap stations, boosting efficiency and supporting clean energy.Battery swapping animations typically depict the process of an electric vehicle (EV) exchanging a depleted battery for a fully charged one at a dedicated station. These animations often show a robotic arm or automated system removing the old battery and installing a new one, minimizing downtime for the EV. The animations may also showcase the charging and storage of depleted batteries at the swapping station.
Elements in Battery Swapping Animations:
EV at a station The animation usually starts with an electric vehicle arriving at a battery swapping station.
Automated process: The animation highlights the automation of the battery exchange, whether it's a robotic arm or a guided system.
removal The animation depicts the removal of the depleted battery from the EV.
Battery insertion: The animation then shows the insertion of a fully charged battery.
